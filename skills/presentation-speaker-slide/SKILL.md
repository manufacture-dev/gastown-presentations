# Presentation Speaker Slide

## Goal

Create speaker slides that feel professional and useful without becoming resumes.

## Workflow

1. Gather speaker inputs:
   - name
   - role
   - organization
   - short bio
   - photo
   - links
2. Crop photos consistently, usually centered on faces.
3. Keep bios short and presentation-relevant.
4. Add QR codes only when they add value.
5. Include readable text links near QR codes when appropriate.
6. Align speaker cards consistently.
7. Verify the slide in browser/export to ensure:
   - faces are not awkwardly cropped
   - text fits
   - QR codes are scannable
   - links are readable

## Content Guidance

Use one or two concise credibility points per speaker. Avoid long career summaries.
